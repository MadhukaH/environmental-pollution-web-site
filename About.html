Revolutionizing Waste Management: Hambantota's Premier Recycling Hub

I gathered all the necessary photos and knowledge about waste management for my project from this facility, conveniently located near the Hambantota Solar Power Plant. At this site, waste from the Hambantota and Suriyawewa areas is brought in and meticulously sorted into proper piles for recycling. The primary objective is to transform this waste into compost and ensure its disposal doesn't harm the environment. With approximately 50 dedicated employees, all financially supported by the government, the facility operates efficiently to fulfill its environmental mission. The establishment of more such facilities nationwide promises a brighter future for our environment, fostering cleaner surroundings for all.
